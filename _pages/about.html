Vision

About our Vision

The prospects about the AI-Revolution can be overwhelming - but although the tension is palpable, the accompanied challenges are as well!

From an individual perspective this concerns enterprises, scientific workgroups and government agencies, as it becomes increasingly demanding to keep pace with the accelerated development and to stay competitive. On a larger scale, the global centralization of competencies in data science poses the challenge of counteracting new geo-political and economic dependency structures and thus to preserve freedoms and sovereignty.

Our vision is a democratic and federated AI-Revolution, that does not lead to exclusion or incapacitation, but promotes public well-being, social justice and social cohesion.

Mission

About our Mission and our Projects

For us communication and cooperation are the key factors and driving force behind scientific and industrial progress.

In order to realize our vision, we therefore started to identify the foremost obstacles for collaborations in data science . These considerations led to the foundations of a pioneering software framework, that shapes our mission: The Liquid Code framework.

The Liquid Code framework is a software framework for machine learning and data analysis applications, that interconnects collaborative data science with automated machine learning. Thereby it strictly seperates data sources, algorithms and the program flow, which provides some significant advantages: The program flow and the algorithms can be developed and maintained by independent groups, e.i. by industrial developers and university research groups.

{% include article/figure.html url="/images/fig/liquid-collaboration.svg" description="Collaboration between researchers (green) and developers (blue) in the Liquid Code framework" %}

The key components of the Liquid Code Framework are constituted by three subprojects, that implement the handling of data sources, algorithms and program flow. These components are respectively given by Pandora, Motley and Nemoa and share a common code base, given by the frootlab shared library.

About Us

About us and our convictions

We are a young enthusiastic team of developers and data scientists, which has been formed in mid-2018 in Heidelberg, Germany. Our fundamental conviction is, that research and education should be provided with universal access. Accordingly we release our projects as free software. In order to provide stable development, we keep the sovereignty about the code as a single vendor.
