Vision

About Us and our Vision

The prospects about the AI-Revolution can be overwhelming - but although the tension is palpable, the accompanied challenges are as well!

This concerns enterprises, scientific workgroups and government agencies, as it becomes increasingly demanding to keep pace with the accelerating development and to stay competitive. We want to step into this breach by providing new technologies for the next generation of data science applications.

We are a young developers team, which has been formed in mid-2018 in Heidelberg, Germany. Our fundamental conviction is, that research and education should be provided with universal access. Accordingly we release our projects as free software. In order to provide stable development, we keep the sovereignty about the code as a single vendor.

Mission

About our Mission and our Projects

In order to realize our vision, we started with identifying the foremost obstacles for collaborations in data science. Thereupon we initiated the planning and development of a comprehensive solution, that essentially shapes our mission: The Liquid Code framework.

The Liquid Code framework is a software framework for data analysis applications, that incorporates machine intelligence and collaborative data science into the whole software life cycle. The key components are constituted be three subprojects, that implement a strictly separation of data sources, algorithms and program flow. These components are respectively given by Pandora, Motley and Nemoa and share a common code base, given by the frootlab shared library.
